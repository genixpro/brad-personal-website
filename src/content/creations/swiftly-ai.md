body: "Swiftly AI was an attempt to create a tool that could automate the complicated process of valuing commercial real estate. Swiftly AI took a new approach - instead of developing a model to predict valuations directly (which has been tried before and failed), instead Swiftly AI focused on automating the same process that human commercial appraisers followed, allowing it to produce accurate valuations even in with extreme data scarcity, along with all of the analysis and detail required by the law in its final report."
