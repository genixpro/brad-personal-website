Welcome to Bradley Arsenault AI Services's Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy covers all demos, apps, and projects created by Bradley Arsenault.

This Privacy Policy provides detailed information on how we collect, store, use, and protect the personal information you provide when using our Voice AI system and related services.

At Bradley Arsenault AI Services, we respect the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including your name, email address, telephone number, and details necessary for account creation and transaction processing.

Automatically collected information includes your IP address, browser type, device type, and usage statistics related to how you interact with our services.

Information collected is used to provide and improve our services, manage user accounts, facilitate customer support, transactions, and appointment scheduling.

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, secure servers, and physical security measures.

For questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at brad@bradleyarsenault.me.
